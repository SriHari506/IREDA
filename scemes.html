Our Schemes

IREDA offers a variety of schemes to promote the development of renewable energy projects. Our schemes are designed to provide financial support and incentives to encourage the adoption of clean energy technologies.

Scheme 1: Renewable Energy Development

This scheme focuses on the development of renewable energy projects such as solar, wind, and biomass. Key features include:

  • Low-interest loans for project financing.
  • Subsidies for initial capital investment.
  • Technical support and consultancy services.
  • Priority grid access for renewable energy projects.

Scheme 2: Energy Efficiency Improvement

Our Energy Efficiency Improvement scheme aims to enhance the energy efficiency of industrial and commercial establishments. Benefits include:

  • Financial assistance for energy audits and retrofitting.
  • Incentives for implementing energy-efficient technologies.
  • Training programs for energy management personnel.
  • Support for adopting international energy efficiency standards.

Scheme 3: Rural Electrification

To support rural electrification, IREDA offers schemes that focus on providing clean and sustainable energy solutions to rural areas. Key aspects include:

  • Grants for setting up mini-grids and micro-grids.
  • Subsidized financing for off-grid solar systems.
  • Support for community-based renewable energy projects.
  • Awareness programs on the benefits of renewable energy.

Scheme 4: Research and Development

IREDA's R&D scheme encourages innovation in renewable energy technologies. Highlights of this scheme include:

  • Funding for research projects in renewable energy.
  • Collaboration with academic and research institutions.
  • Support for pilot projects and technology demonstrations.
  • Incentives for patents and innovations in clean energy.

Application Process

Interested applicants can apply for our schemes by submitting a detailed project proposal along with necessary documentation. The application process includes:

  • Initial screening of project proposals.
  • Detailed evaluation by our technical and financial experts.
  • Approval and sanctioning of financial assistance.
  • Regular monitoring and evaluation of project progress.

For more information about our schemes and how to apply, please contact us or visit our official website.
