About Us

The Indian Renewable Energy Development Agency Limited (IREDA) is a Mini Ratna (Category â€“ I) Government of India Enterprise under the administrative control of the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE). Established in 1987, IREDA's mission is to promote, develop, and extend financial assistance for renewable energy and energy efficiency/conservation projects in India.

Our Mission

To be the leading provider of innovative financial solutions for renewable energy projects, contributing to India's sustainable development and energy security.

Our Vision

To enable India's transition to a sustainable and secure energy future through financial and technical support for renewable energy projects.

Our Values

IREDA is committed to:

  • Innovation: Continuously seeking and implementing new ideas and technologies to promote renewable energy.
  • Sustainability: Ensuring that all projects contribute to environmental, economic, and social sustainability.
  • Integrity: Maintaining the highest standards of ethics and transparency in all our operations.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest levels of quality and efficiency in our services and processes.
  • Collaboration: Working closely with stakeholders, partners, and communities to achieve common goals.

Our Team

IREDA is led by a team of experienced professionals dedicated to advancing renewable energy development in India. Our team comprises experts in finance, engineering, project management, and policy who work together to provide comprehensive support for renewable energy projects.

Our Impact

Over the years, IREDA has played a crucial role in financing and supporting a wide range of renewable energy projects across India, including solar, wind, hydro, and biomass. Our efforts have contributed to the addition of significant renewable energy capacity to the national grid, reducing greenhouse gas emissions, and promoting sustainable development.
