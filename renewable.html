Renewable Power Information

IREDA Navaratna is dedicated to the development and promotion of renewable energy sources to ensure a sustainable future. We focus on various renewable energy technologies to reduce our carbon footprint and provide clean energy solutions. Here are some key areas of our renewable power initiatives:

Solar Power

Solar power harnesses the energy of the sun to generate electricity. IREDA supports the development of solar power projects, offering financing and technical assistance for:

  • Photovoltaic (PV) systems
  • Solar thermal power plants
  • Rooftop solar installations
  • Solar water heaters

Wind Power

Wind power is one of the fastest-growing renewable energy sources. IREDA provides support for wind power projects, including:

  • Onshore wind farms
  • Offshore wind farms
  • Small and medium-sized wind turbines

Hydro Power

Hydro power utilizes the energy of flowing water to generate electricity. Our hydro power initiatives include:

  • Large-scale hydroelectric power plants
  • Small and micro hydro projects
  • Pumped storage hydroelectricity

Biomass Energy

Biomass energy is produced from organic materials, providing a reliable and sustainable energy source. IREDA supports biomass projects such as:

  • Biogas plants
  • Biomass power generation
  • Waste-to-energy projects

Geothermal Energy

Geothermal energy harnesses the heat from within the Earth to generate power. Our geothermal energy projects focus on:

  • Geothermal power plants
  • Direct-use geothermal applications
  • Geothermal heat pumps

Innovative Renewable Solutions

IREDA is also involved in exploring and supporting innovative renewable energy solutions, including:

  • Hybrid renewable energy systems
  • Energy storage technologies
  • Smart grid solutions

By investing in these renewable energy technologies, IREDA aims to contribute significantly to India's clean energy goals and ensure a sustainable and environmentally friendly energy future.
